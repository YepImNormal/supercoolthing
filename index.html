Prom?

Dear Neeshi,

In the dance of life, our paths intertwined,

Last year's missteps, in the past confined.

With honesty and a heart now clear,

Will you be my prom date, my dear?

Your smile is the melody, your laughter, the rhyme,

So, Neeshi, will you make my prom the perfect time?

And be my prom date?

Wrong answer! Give it another shot.
